# BeoOS brand pack

BeoOS is an AI-powered business communication operating system for service businesses. The brand should feel practical, sharp, warm, and trustworthy: a serious business tool that still feels human enough for Nigerian founders, studio owners, retailers, and brick-and-mortar operators.

## Logo assets

Primary assets live in:

- `frontend/public/favicon.svg` — browser favicon and small app icon.
- `frontend/public/brand/beoos-mark.svg` — primary square logo mark.
- `frontend/public/brand/beoos-mark-light.svg` — square mark for light layouts.
- `frontend/public/brand/beoos-horizontal-dark.svg` — horizontal logo for light backgrounds.
- `frontend/public/brand/beoos-horizontal-light.svg` — horizontal logo for dark backgrounds.
- `frontend/public/brand/beoos-wordmark.svg` — simple text-only wordmark.

The favicon-style mark is the core identity: a warm coral BeoOS tile inside a dark rounded system frame. Use it anywhere space is tight: favicon, PWA icon, dashboard avatar, social profile, Meta app icon, and mobile shortcut.

## Brand idea

**One command center for the messages, leads, quotes, and follow-ups that keep a business alive.**

BeoOS should not sound like a generic chatbot. It should sound like a calm operator sitting beside the business owner, helping them catch every serious opportunity.

## Typography

Use a modern system stack until a custom font decision is made:

```css
font-family: Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif;
```

Headlines should be heavy and compact. Body copy should be plain and easy to understand. BeoOS is a productivity tool, so clarity beats cleverness.

## Voice

BeoOS copy should be:

- direct, not corporate;
- confident, not arrogant;
- warm, not playful to the point of distraction;
- specific, not vague.

Good:

> Connect Zoho, Gmail, WhatsApp, and website forms into one business inbox.

Avoid:

> Revolutionize your omnichannel digital experience with cutting-edge AI.

## Founder story summary

BeoOS was founded by Benjamin Odeke after experiencing the pressure of running multiple businesses at once while juggling customer messages, quotes, follow-ups, and different software tools. The product exists because small and growing businesses need serious automation without paying for five separate enterprise platforms before they can grow.

## Usage rules

- Keep enough clear space around the logo. Minimum clear space should match the height of the `B` in the mark.
- Use the coral mark on warm canvas or dark navy backgrounds.
- Do not stretch, rotate, recolor randomly, or place the mark on busy images.
- For Meta, Vercel, Clerk, Railway, and social profiles, use the square mark.
- For the website header, pitch decks, PDFs, invoices, and proposal covers, use the horizontal logo.

## Product positioning

BeoOS sits between:

- shared inbox tools;
- CRM systems;
- WhatsApp automation;
- quotation/proposal tools;
- AI assistants;
- follow-up schedulers.

The difference is that BeoOS is built around the real workflow of a service business: request comes in, AI understands it, lead is scored, quote is prepared, owner approves, customer is followed up, and the business sees what is working.

## Starter pricing language

Launch pricing can stay simple for Nigeria:

- Starter — ₦5,000/month for small businesses testing unified inbox and basic AI assistance.
- Growth — ₦15,000/month for active businesses using CRM, quotes, follow-ups, and multiple channels.
- Scale — ₦30,000/month for teams with higher message volume, multiple businesses, and advanced automation.

Keep pricing usage-aware later so heavy AI, WhatsApp, email, and notification usage can be billed fairly.
