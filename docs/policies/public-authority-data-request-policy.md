# Public-authority data request policy

BeoOS will not disclose user or Platform Data to a public authority unless the request is written, legally valid and issued by an authority with proper jurisdiction. Every request will be reviewed for legality and scope. BeoOS will challenge requests considered unlawful or excessive where legally permitted, disclose only the minimum information legally required, and document the request, review, response, legal basis and people involved.

## Operating procedure

1. Record the requesting authority, jurisdiction, date received, deadline, legal basis, and requested scope.
2. Verify that the request is written, legally valid, and issued by an authority with proper jurisdiction.
3. Limit internal access to the request to the people required to assess and respond to it.
4. Challenge or narrow unlawful, excessive, unclear, or overbroad requests where legally permitted.
5. Disclose only the minimum information legally required.
6. Document the final response, disclosed data categories, legal basis, reviewer, approver, and delivery method.
7. Preserve the request and response record for compliance and audit purposes.
